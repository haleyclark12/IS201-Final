Fun Facts

  • Seattle is the birthplace of Starbucks! The original Starbucks first opened in 1971 at Pike Place Market.
  • The Space Needle was built for the 1962 World Fair to serve as the main centerpiece of the event.
  • Contrary to popular belief, Seattle is not the rainiest city in the United States!
  • Many famous movies and TV shows were filmed in Seattle, including 10 Things I Hate About You, Grey's Anatomy, and Sleepless in Seattle.
